The Excursions page helps you plan, review, and manage school trips and activities. It brings together planning details, people, documents, tasks, approvals, and risk information in one place.

## What You Can Do

- View all excursions in your workspace.
- Filter and browse excursions by status or planning stage.
- Create a quick draft when you only need to capture the basics.
- Start a new excursion using a guided builder.
- Open an excursion to review the full planning workspace.
- Manage overview details, itinerary information, staffing, participants, transport, forms, documents, emails, and website details.
- Track linked tasks and activity for an excursion.
- Review risk management information and approval progress.
- Prepare information for parents, staff, and reviewers.

## Common Workflows

### Create a new excursion

Start with a quick draft if you are still gathering details, or use the guided builder when you are ready to work through the main planning steps.

### Review an excursion before approval

Open the excursion and check the relevant tabs, including overview, participants, staffing, risk management, documents, and reviews.

### Keep planning work together

Use the excursion detail page as the central place for related tasks, forms, emails, files, comments, and activity.

## Tips

- Draft excursions are useful for capturing early ideas before the plan is complete.
- Use tabs on the excursion detail page to focus on one planning area at a time.
- Keep participant, staffing, transport, and risk details up to date before sharing information more widely.
