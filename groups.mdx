title: "Groups"
---

The Groups page helps you organise students into useful collections, such as classes, year levels, houses, programs, excursion groups, or activity groups.

## What You Can Do

- View all groups in your workspace.
- Create a new group.
- Edit group details.
- Open a group to see its students.
- Add students to a group.
- Transfer or adjust group memberships when students move.
- Use groups to support planning, communication, and participation tracking.

## Common Workflows

### Create a class or activity group

Add a group with a clear name, then add the students who belong to it.

### Review group membership

Open a group to check which students are included before using it for planning or communication.

### Move students between groups

Use membership tools when students change class, program, or activity grouping.

## Tips

- Choose group names that staff will recognise easily.
- Review group membership before relying on a group for excursion planning.
- Keep groups up to date when student enrolments or class assignments change.
