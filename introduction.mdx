AnySchool.ai is an excursion management platform for schools. You use it to plan trips, manage participants and staff, collect permissions, handle risk, and keep everyone aligned.

## Who this is for

These docs are written for staff who plan and run school excursions: coordinators, teachers, and administrators who work in the product day to day.

## How the docs are structured

- **Get started** — Orientation and a short path to first value (this section and **Quickstart**).
- **Excursions** — End-to-end guidance for a single excursion, including each tab on the excursion record.
- **Dashboard** — Workspace-wide overview and follow-up.
- **Features** — Deeper reference for tasks, forms, people, locations, vendors, risk bank, and related screens.

When you are ready, open **Quickstart** for a compact checklist of common first steps.
