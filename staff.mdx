---
title: "Staff"
---

The Staff page helps you view workspace members and understand staff involvement across school activities. It is useful for checking who has access, reviewing staff records, and seeing excursion participation.

## What You Can Do

- Browse staff members in the workspace.
- Open a staff member's summary page.
- Review a staff member's excursion history where available.
- Check staff details for planning and coordination.
- Use staff records when assigning responsibilities or reviewing past involvement.
- Keep a shared view of who is connected to the workspace.

## Common Workflows

### Find a staff member

Use the staff list to locate a workspace member and open their summary if you need more context.

### Review excursion involvement

Open a staff member's page to understand their history with excursions and related activities.

### Coordinate staffing

Use staff information when planning supervision, roles, or responsibilities for upcoming excursions.

## Tips

- Check staff records when confirming excursion staffing arrangements.
- Use the summary page for context before assigning or reviewing responsibilities.
- Keep workspace membership accurate so the staff directory remains useful.
