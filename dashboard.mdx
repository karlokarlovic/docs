The Dashboard gives you a quick view of what is happening across your workspace. Use it as the starting point for checking excursion progress, outstanding work, and recent activity.

## What You Can Do

- See a high-level summary of excursions, including active, draft, and review-ready work.
- Open the excursions list when you need to review or update planned activities.
- Check live or upcoming excursions from a central overview.
- Review tasks that may need your attention.
- Spot items that need follow-up, such as unfinished drafts or outstanding actions.
- See recent workspace activity so you can understand what has changed.

## Common Workflows

### Start your day

Open the Dashboard to see which excursions are active, which items need attention, and whether any recent updates affect your work.

### Follow up on planning

Use the excursion summary and attention areas to find trips that may still need details, review, or team follow-up.

### Move quickly to the right area

Use the Dashboard as a launch point for excursions, tasks, and recent updates instead of checking each page separately.

## Tips

- Check the Dashboard before approving or publishing excursion information.
- Use recent activity to understand changes made by other staff members.
- Treat the Dashboard as a summary; open the relevant page for detailed editing.
