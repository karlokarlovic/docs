The Risk Management Bank is where your school maintains reusable risk groups, hazards, and controls that can be applied to excursions.

## What this screen is for

- Browse the bank as a tree.
- Create and maintain reusable risk structures.
- Tune what information is visible while browsing.

## What you can see

- Page title **Risk Management Bank** with subtitle **Create reusable risk groups, hazards, and controls that can flow into excursions in linked mode.**
- A **New Risk Group** action.
- A **View Options** menu with a **Show in tree** section containing toggles for:
  - **Description**
  - **Lead responsible role**
  - **Severity**
  - **Likelihood**
- Loading and error states:
  - **Loading risk bank...**
  - **Unable to load the risk bank** with an error message
- When the bank has content, an information card titled **Linked inheritance ready** explains how linked items behave when reused on excursions.
- When there are no groups yet, an empty message encourages creating the first group.

## Common actions

### Create your first structure

Use **New Risk Group** to start a top-level group, then add hazards and controls from the tree menus.

### Tune the tree display

Use **View Options** to show more detail directly in the tree when you need a quick scan without opening each item.

## Permissions and read-only behaviour

Some controls may remain visible even when your workspace role cannot perform writes. If an action is not permitted, saving or destructive actions should fail with a clear error message.

## Tips

- Build bank content for patterns you reuse every year (transport, water, overnight stays).
- Keep hazard titles short so searching and scanning stays easy.
- Turn on likelihood and severity in the tree when reviewing a group for consistency.
