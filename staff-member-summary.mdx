The staff summary page is a read-only view focused on a single staff member’s excursion participation, with an admin-only compliance editor.

## What this screen is for

- Confirm who the staff member is.
- Review excursion history and how the staff member participated.
- Maintain compliance training metadata when you are authorised.

## What you can see

- A header using the staff member’s display name.
- A subtitle explaining the page is a read-only excursion summary.
- A profile card with the staff member name.
- A **Compliance** card.
- An **Excursion history** table.

## Compliance card

The compliance card tracks training currency with pairs of controls:

- **CPR trained** with an **Expiry** date field when enabled
- **Anaphylaxis training** with **Expiry**
- **Administration of medication training** with **Expiry**
- **WWCC** with **Expiry**, plus **WWCC sighted** date tracking and read-only text showing who sighted the WWCC and when (when recorded)

Actions:

- **Reset** and **Save** appear when you are allowed to edit compliance and there are unsaved changes.
- Saving shows a success toast with the staff member’s name.

## Excursion history table

Columns include:

- **Excursion**
- **Status**
- **Staff role**
- **Attending** (including attending vs not attending filters)
- **Notes**
- **Updated**

Clicking a row opens the related excursion.

Filtering includes excursion search plus multi-select filters where supported.

## Empty, loading, and error states

- While loading the summary, you see **Loading staff summary...**
- If the summary cannot load, you see **Failed to load staff summary** with a message.
- If history filters exclude all rows, you see **No excursions match the current filters.**

## Permissions and read-only behaviour

The excursion summary is read-only for normal browsing.

Compliance editing is restricted to authorised administrators. If you are not authorised, controls are disabled.

## Tips

- Update compliance as soon as training renews so excursion planning stays trustworthy.
- Use excursion history to confirm recent involvement before assigning responsibilities.
