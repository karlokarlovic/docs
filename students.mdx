The Students page is the main roster for student records in your workspace. It helps staff find student details, maintain accurate records, and connect students with parents or carers.

## What You Can Do

- Browse the student roster.
- Create a new student record.
- Edit student details.
- Import student records when adding many students at once.
- Manage relationships between students and parents or carers.
- Use student information for groups, excursions, forms, and planning.
- Keep student records current as details change.

## Common Workflows

### Add a student

Create a student record when a new student needs to be available for groups, excursions, or family relationships.

### Import a student list

Use import when you have a larger list of students to add or update.

### Link a student to parents or carers

Open relationship management for the student and connect the appropriate parent or carer records.

- Review student relationships before sending family communication.
