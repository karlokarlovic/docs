Use this page as a fast path into the product. You can return here whenever you onboard someone new.

## 1. Open the Dashboard

Start from the **Dashboard** to see workspace activity, excursions that need attention, and tasks to follow up.

## 2. Create or open an excursion

From your excursions list, open an existing trip or start a new one. The **Overview** tab is the main summary for that excursion.

## 3. Add people and logistics

Typical early steps:

- Use **Participants** to attach students and track permission status.
- Use **Staffing** to add staff and roles.
- Use **Timeline** for the schedule and **Transport** when you need movement between places.

## 4. Forms and communication

Connect or build **Forms** for permissions and surveys, and use **Emails** when you need to message families or staff from the excursion.

## 5. Risk and approvals

Use **Risk management** to record hazards and controls, and **Reviews** when your school uses an approval workflow.

## Next steps

Browse the **Excursions** section for tab-by-tab detail, or jump into **Features** for workspace-wide tools like tasks, locations, and the student roster.
