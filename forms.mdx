The Forms page helps you create and manage forms for collecting information from families, staff, or other participants. Forms can support excursion planning, permissions, surveys, and general information gathering.

## What You Can Do

- View all forms in your workspace.
- Create a new form.
- Open a form to design or update its questions.
- Build forms with fields that match the information you need to collect.
- Use forms alongside excursions when extra information or responses are required.
- Keep reusable form templates available for future activities.
- Review forms before sharing them with your audience.

## Common Workflows

### Create a permission or information form

Create a new form, give it a clear name, then add the fields you need in the form builder.

### Update an existing form

Open the form from the list and adjust the questions, wording, or structure before it is used again.

### Prepare a form for an excursion

Build the questions needed for the excursion, such as permissions, dietary details, medical information, or volunteer availability.

## Tips

- Keep form titles short and easy for families or staff to recognise.
- Group related questions together so the form is easier to complete.
- Review wording before sharing a form to make sure instructions are clear.
